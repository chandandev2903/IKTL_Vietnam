Voice Bot Solutions in Vietnam

Smarter Customer Conversations with AI-Powered Voice Bots

In Vietnam, regulated and high-volume industries— from banking/financial services to e-commerce, logistics, healthcare, utilities, and telecom —need fast, consistent, and compliant customer interactions. Human-only teams struggle with spikes in demand, routine FAQs, and time-sensitive reminders.

Ikontel’s AI Voice Bots automate inbound and outbound calls in Vietnamese and English , reducing wait times and operational costs while keeping communications professional and compliant with SBV (for financial use cases) and MIC (telecom) guidelines. Specialty: Proven call flows for debt recovery & collections — without limiting you to that use case.

Contact Us

What is a Voice Bot?

An AI-driven system that makes or answers calls and understands
natural language to complete tasks like reminders, confirmations,
escalations, and data capture.

Where It’s Used
  • Debt Collection & Recovery (specialty): Personalized past-due reminders, promise-to-pay capture, agent transfer.
  • Customer Service : Balance/benefit queries, order status, delivery updates, policy information.
  • Onboarding & KYC Reminders : Document follow-ups, activation nudges.
  • Renewals & Retention : Policy renewals, subscription reminders, usage alerts.
  • Ops & CX Notifications : Service outages/restoration updates, appointment reminders, satisfaction callbacks.

Why Vietnam Enterprises Choose Voice Automation

Handle high volumes without adding headcount.

Lower agent fatigue & cost by automating repeat calls.

Maintain SBV/MIC-aligned call flows and recordings.

Key Features

  • Vietnamese & English support; custom phrasing per industry.
  • Compliance-ready scripts and call recording where applicable.
  • CRM/Collections/Order-Mgmt integration for real-time updates
  • Scalable outbound campaigns; smart retry & time-of-day rules.
  • Audit-friendly logs & encryption.
