Collection Made Life Easy – Smart Loan Recovery for Vietnam

In Vietnam, the financial sector is undergoing a major transformation. With the rapid growth of consumer lending, microfinance, and digital banking , the number of borrowers has multiplied significantly. While this expansion creates new opportunities for banks and NBFCs, it also brings a critical challenge— loan defaults. Managing collections efficiently has become one of the top priorities for lenders in Vietnam.

Traditional loan recovery methods often rely on manual calling, field agents, and repeated SMS reminders. These approaches are slow, costly, and ineffective in today’s digital-first environment. Customers expect convenience, automation, and clear reminders through channels they already use, such as MoMo, Zalo, and mobile apps .

That’s why Ikontel introduces Collection Made Life Easy , a cutting-edge AI-powered platform designed to simplify and automate loan recovery in Vietnam .

Why Loan Collection Needs a Modern Approach in Vietnam

Vietnam’s lending ecosystem is growing fast. From BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later) schemes to personal loans and credit cards, customers are borrowing more than ever before. However, rising borrowing also means higher risks of overdue payments.

Many lenders struggle because :
  • Manual follow-ups are slow and inefficient
  • Borrowers are scattered across different cities & provinces
  • Customers prefer mobile-first communication
  • Compliance with SBV (State Bank of Vietnam) regulations is mandatory

Real-World Use Case

Imagine a borrower in Hanoi who misses an EMI payment. Instead of waiting for a call center agent, they receive an automated Zalo message with a payment link. If they still don’t respond, an AI voicebot calls them in Vietnamese to remind them. With just one click, they repay through MoMo .
This seamless flow saves time for the lender and improves convenience for the customer.
