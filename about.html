Who We Are

We provide perfect IT solutions & AI technology

Ikontel is a global leader in AI-powered communication solutions with a rich history of delivering cutting-edge products and services to businesses worldwide. Since its inception in 2011, Ikontel has grown from a small product development company to an industry powerhouse, trusted by over 500 clients across multiple sectors, including banking, agriculture, utility, healthcare, and government.


With a strong foundation built on innovation, automation, and AI-first technology, Ikontel continues to drive advancements in communication and debt recovery through its flagship platform Hybrify Connecta. In 2023, Ikontel expanded its operations to Vietnam, bringing our proven expertise to the rapidly growing financial services market in Vietnam.

Meet Our Visionaries


Sanjay Rout


Sanjay Rout, as the Founder & CEO, has led Ikontel with a focus on continuous innovation and global expansion. His deep understanding of the telecom industry and passion for AI technology have been key drivers in the company’s success.


Sanjit Pradhan


Sanjit Pradhan, Co-Founder & CTO, brings over two decades of expertise in technology development and AI systems. His technical vision is the backbone of Ikontel’s AI-powered debt recovery platform, which has gained significant traction in India and is now expanding to Vietnam.

We run all kinds of IT services

Ikontel specializes in providing AI-driven debt collection and communication services that help businesses recover loans more efficiently while enhancing customer satisfaction. Our solutions are designed for a range of industries, particularly banking, fintech, healthcare, and telecom, and include:

Hybrify Connecta

Our product automates loan collection, including multi-channel communication (SMS, email, voice calls, WhatsApp, and more) and AI bots that engage borrowers in real-time, leading to faster recovery and better experience.

AI & Automation

We provide a suite of services such as AI voice bots, IVR systems, cloud telephony, missed call services, robocalls, and SMS gateways to ensure seamless and scalable communication across all customer touchpoints.

Cloud-Based Solutions

Our cloud dialer and cloud telephony systems enable businesses to scale communication efforts efficiently without compromising on quality or compliance.

AI Chatbots

These bots engage customers in meaningful conversations across multiple channels, from loan recovery reminders to customer support, all while ensuring compliance with local regulations.
