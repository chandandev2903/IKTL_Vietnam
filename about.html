Who We Are

We provide perfect IT solutions & AI technology for any startups.

Ikontel is a global leader in AI-powered communication solutions with a rich history of delivering cutting-edge products and services to businesses worldwide. Since its inception in 2011, Ikontel has grown from a small product development company to an industry powerhouse, trusted by over 500 clients across multiple sectors, including banking, telecom, healthcare, and government.


With a strong foundation built on innovation, automation, and AI-first technology, Ikontel continues to drive advancements in communication and debt recovery through its flagship platform Hybrify Connecta. In 2023, Ikontel expanded its operations to Vietnam, bringing our proven expertise to the rapidly growing financial services market in Southeast Asia

Meet Our Visionaries

Sanjay Rout

Sanjay Rout, as the Founder & CEO, has led Ikontel with a focus on continuous innovation and global expansion. His deep understanding of the telecom industry and passion for AI technology have been key drivers in the company’s success.

Sanjit Pradhan

Sanjit Pradhan, Co-Founder & CTO, brings over two decades of expertise in technology development and AI systems. His technical vision is the backbone of Ikontel’s AI-powered debt recovery platform, which has gained significant traction in India and is now expanding to Vietnam.

Since our inception, Ikontel has delivered real-time automation solutions that help businesses save costs, improve operational efficiency, and provide a superior customer experience. In India, Ikontel processes over 1 million calls per day, and our services have helped businesses reduce costs by up to 90% and improve manpower efficiency by 70%.

One of our notable contributions was during the COVID-19 pandemic, when we supported the Government of India in managing and coordinating public health communications, reaching millions of people daily.

Vision: To be the global leader in AI-powered communication and debt recovery solutions, driving the digital transformation of customer engagement and operational automation across industries.

Mission: To deliver reliable, scalable, and cost-efficient AI-powered solutions that help businesses in Vietnam and beyond automate their operations, reduce costs, and improve customer satisfaction.

Ikontel is committed to ensuring that businesses are equipped with the most advanced technology to navigate the digital landscape, build customer loyalty, and drive growth.

The launch of Ikontel Vietnam marks a new chapter in our global journey. With Vietnam’s booming fintech and banking sector, we see a massive opportunity to help businesses transition to digital debt recovery solutions. Our goal is to empower Vietnamese financial institutions with the tools they need to recover loans more effectively, engage borrowers across multiple touchpoints, and automate their operations to save time and costs.

Our team in Vietnam works directly with local banks, fintechs, and telecom providers to ensure that our solutions are not only cutting-edge but also tailored to the unique needs and challenges of the Vietnamese market. By providing Vietnamese language support, we are able to offer a more personalized experience for borrowers, ensuring higher engagement and better results.
